# Al-Furqan Platform Website

**Learn the Qur'an anywhere, anytime!**

Al-Furqan Platform is an interactive website that helps users learn Qur'an recitation, Tajweed rules, and Islamic knowledge in an easy and engaging way.

---

## What You Can Do

- **Explore Supplications (Doaa)**  
  - Browse images and videos of Islamic supplications.  
  - Click to view in fullscreen for a better experience.

- **Read the Latest News**  
  - Stay updated with announcements and platform updates.  
  - Each news item includes images and detailed descriptions.

- **See Testimonials**  
  - Read what users say about their experience learning with Al-Furqan.  
  - Includes star ratings and personal stories.

- **Download the Mobile App**  
  - Get the Al-Furqan app for iOS and Android.  
  - Learn Qur'an on the go with interactive lessons.

- **Connect with Us**  
  - Contact info and social media links available in the footer.  
  - Learn more about our programs, services, and online classes.

---

## How to Use

1. Open the website in any browser.  
2. Navigate through sections: Doaa, News, Testimonials, or Download App.  
3. Click on images or videos to view them fullscreen.  
4. Use sliders to scroll through content easily.
